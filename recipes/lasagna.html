Lasagna

    Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato products: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg

    Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest befofre serving.
