Fried Mozzarella Cheese Sticks

    Ingredients

  • Eggs.
  • Garlic.
  • Salt.
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch

    Steps

  1. In three separate bowls: Mix the eggs and water, the bread crumbs and garlic salt, and flour and cornstarch.
  2. Heat the oil to 365 degrees F in a heavy saucepan. One at a time, coat each mozzarella stick in the flour mixture, then the egg mixture, then in the garlic-spiked bread crumbs. Drop the sticks into the hot oil and fry for about 30 seconds, or until golden-brown. Drain the fried mozzarella sticks on paper towels.
